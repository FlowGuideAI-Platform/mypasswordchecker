Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

Plans are billed monthly. Overage charges are calculated at the end of each billing cycle. You'll receive an invoice via email.

Can I change plans?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my quota?

Free plan requests are blocked. Paid plans allow overage at $0.0125 per request, automatically billed at month-end.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on your first month. Contact support for refund requests.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! The Free plan includes 50 requests per month forever. No credit card required to start.
